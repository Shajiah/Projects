Instructions:

1. Enter the name of the item, the price, and how many items of that type you want and click add

2. If you put invalid numbers for the price or quantity, an error message will appear

3. Item with the correct price total will appear in the cart on the right. 

4. The items in your cart will be alphabetized 

5. You can remove the last item in the cart by clicking the remove button
